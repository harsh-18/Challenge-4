    Telemetry Ingestion

    Monitors gate sensor volumes, public transit bridge pressure, weather radar feeds, and elevator elevator logs.

    2

    SOP Matcher (RAG)

    Retrieves matches from vector index of approved FIFA venue security guides, host city policies, and translation lexicons.

    3

    Contextual Reasoner

    Constructs structured instruction prompts citing specific map gates, lift numbers, and language translations.

    4

    Human Approval Loop

    Requires an operator's approval for high-risk steward instructions, pushing to public PA systems, or dispatching responders.
